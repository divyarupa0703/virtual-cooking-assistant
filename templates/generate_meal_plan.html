Chicken Curry

Ingredients: chicken, coconut milk, curry paste, rice

Instructions: Heat oil in a pan. Add chicken and cook until browned. Add curry paste and coconut milk. Simmer until chicken is cooked through. Serve with rice.

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Nutritional Info: Calories: 400, Protein: 25g, Fat: 15g, Carbs: 30g

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients: mixed vegetables, tofu, soy sauce, sesame oil, rice

Instructions: Heat sesame oil in a wok or pan. Add tofu and stir-fry until golden. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender. Season with soy sauce. Serve with rice.

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Nutritional Info: Calories: 350, Protein: 20g, Fat: 10g, Carbs: 40g
